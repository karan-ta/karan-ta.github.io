Google adsense.
AH.

The shining light to a beginner writer.
This is the reason i kept my blog on blogger.
as i already had 0.23$ in my adsense account :)
I could have shifted my blog to github with jekyll
or I could have gone the self hosted route as well.

But i decided to stick to blogger to save my 0.23$ and possibly grow my adsense account balance.

After 2 weeks of this decision - I am happy with blogger so far.
It allowed me a custom domain.
When i find time - I can modify my theme as well when I find time.

But adsense is not making me happy at all so far.
The reasons are as below:

1) The adsense revenue daily is not consistent with page views.

2) I am worried that i am stealing money from other businesses
If someone clicks on an ad on my blog and visits the advertiser's business web page - I get paid.
But am i ensuring business for that click ?
so are businesses loosing money when someone clicks on an ad on my blog by mistake ?
This makes me uneasy  that i am stealing money from advertisers.
I would be happy if I knew that clicks that get an advertiser business are only being charged for.
