I Attended mumbai tech meet on 18th may held at Direct I .

This time there were only python talks from the mumpy google group.
I had volunteered to give a talk on Introduction to Data Mining with python . 

I spoke about a code walk through to scrape the election results on the election commission of india website . 

I had planned to create a simple app to display the top 4 candidiates of each constituency .
And then display the summary of news from different news sources for these candidiates . 

I started coding the night before (at 11 pm ) the event as throughout the week i was occupied with choiceman tasks and also had to do multiple follow ups with our sofa guy to deliver our first sofa in the new apartment we moved into around 4 months back . 


I only managed to create a json with the top 4 candidiates using python till 5 AM .
Till about 1 AM i was studying what works MP , MLA are supposed to do and which news sources I should scrape and how i can get the photos of all the top 4 candidiates to display in frontend app .
Then i started writing a simple frontend and was targeting to complete it by 10 am .

Suddenly from 5 am things started getting super stuck :)
I thought I should avoid flask , django as i just wanted to display data from json in my html file . so i started using simple python web server for this .

But I just couldnt get the simple web server to render images .
Time went by and suddenly it became 10 am and panic started hitting me :)

I decided to use flask but from long time had not bootstrapped a new project so wasted some more time debugging and didnt read the docs .

Finally had to give up on the frontend app .

I made a list of all the concepts , ideas i had studied earlier  and thought i would explain them all at the event .

I was super cranky at the talk and the code walk through turned out to be rather mundane :)
Better planning , execution and luck for next time .
