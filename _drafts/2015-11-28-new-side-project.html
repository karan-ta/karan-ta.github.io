Hello friends  !


Today I am starting a new side project called QuickSubmit.

Quicksubmit allows you to submit your business , service , matrimony profile to multiple sites by filling up just one single form .

In this way you get more visibility , response for your listing .

the responses you get can then help you to decide which premium listings you would prefer to buy .

The idea is to help users to easily fill out forms , create profiles from their existing social data and submit to multiple sites .
Then based on the responses , analytics we can decide which premium listings will be good to buy .

I do not have major expectations from economic standpoint as i assume this will be a quick 1 week side project .

Hoping to blog about it and have fun too .
